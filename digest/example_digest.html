Why Recurring Dream Themes?

On Thursday:

Federal Futarchy

On Tuesday:

Futarchy is a new governance model that uses financial markets to evaluate outcomes of policy decisions. It has potential applications in the US government, focusing on measuring various metrics for big policies, small common decisions, agency-specific metrics, and project-specific metrics to enhance decision-making and reduce corruption.

On Friday:
